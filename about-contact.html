My Story

Years ago, I gave up being an Electronic Engineer because of lack of opportunities in my country so I started learning web development. My journey wasn’t easy, my story emphasizes a new problem in programming: knowing what to learn these days, there are numerous resources on a variety of topics & frameworks. Which programming language should I learn? Should I learn Big Data or UI Development? These were the challenges I had struggled with, but once I found helpful communities such as the Odin Project & FreeCodeCamp, I started making progress.

I had heard of WordPress before but never really dug into it. This change when I decided that it’s time to broaden my skillset after facing the numerous failures being an Electronic Engineer, after that I started working with Right Click Software House as a WordPress Developer so I could make my design projects come to live websites.
